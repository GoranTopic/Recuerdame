Linda passed away peacefully after a 3 year long battle with breast cancer on November 1st, 2013. She was a devoted mother to Sara, and Jeffrey and wife of Bill Hughes. The family would like to thank the staff and doctors at Chicago general for taking such great care of her during her time of need.
